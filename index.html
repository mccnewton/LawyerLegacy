Legal Services

Specialized estate planning and probate services designed to protect your family and your legacy.

Wills & Estate Planning

Comprehensive will preparation and estate planning to ensure your wishes are carried out and your loved ones are protected.

Probate Administration

Guiding families through the probate process with compassion, making it as simple and painless as possible.

Applications for Heirship

When a loved one dies without a will, I help families get the determination of heirship needed to settle the estate.

Powers of Attorney

Durable powers of attorney for financial matters and healthcare decisions to protect you and your family.

Guardianship Applications

Legal assistance with guardianship applications to ensure proper care and protection for those who need it most.

Small Estate Affidavits

Simplified processes for smaller estates to help families access assets without full probate proceedings.
