Wills & Estates

Comprehensive will preparation and estate planning to ensure your wishes are carried out and your loved ones are protected.

What's Included:

  • Last Will and Testament preparation
  • Asset distribution planning
  • Guardian designation for minor children
  • Executor appointment guidance

Why You Need It:

Without a properly executed will, Texas intestacy laws determine how your assets are distributed, which may not align with your wishes. A well-crafted will ensures:

  • Your assets go to the people you choose
  • Guardians are appointed for minor children
  • The probate process is simplified
  • Family disputes are minimized

Applications for Heirship

When a loved one dies without a will, Sharon helps families get the determination of heirship needed to settle the estate.

When You Need This:

  • Deceased person left no valid will
  • Need to establish legal heirs
  • Real estate needs to be transferred
  • Bank accounts or other assets require heir verification
  • Family relationships need legal clarification

The Process:

A court proceeding where we present evidence to establish who the legal heirs are under Texas law:

  • Gather family history and documentation
  • Prepare and file the application
  • Present evidence to the court
  • Obtain the official judgment of heirship
Timeline Expectation

This process is highly variable as it mandates the involvement of additional parties who are not bound by any timeline (3 to 8 months or more)

Small Estate Affidavits

Simplified processes for smaller estates to help families access assets without full probate proceedings.

Qualifications:

  • Estate value under $75,000 (excluding homestead and exempt property)
  • No will exists or will doesn't require probate
  • 30 days have passed since death
  • No probate application is pending
  • All legal heirs must agree to and sign the affidavit, as well as two qualified witnesses

Benefits:

Small estate affidavits offer a faster, more cost-effective alternative to formal probate:

  • Avoid lengthy probate proceedings
  • Reduce legal costs significantly
  • Access assets more quickly
  • Simplified court procedures
Timeline Expectation
1 Estate qualification review & documentation (1-2 weeks)
2 Affidavit preparation & heir signatures (1-2 weeks)
3 Asset collection & distribution (1-3 weeks)
3-7 Weeks
