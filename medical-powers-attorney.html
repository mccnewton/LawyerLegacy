Medical Powers of Attorney and Advance Directives

Medical powers of attorney and advance directives to ensure your healthcare wishes are honored

Medical powers of attorney and advance directives to ensure your healthcare preferences and medical decisions are respected when you cannot speak for yourself.

Medical Documents You Need:

  • Medical Power of Attorney for healthcare decisions
  • Directive to Physicians (Living Will)
  • HIPAA Authorization for medical information
  • Mental Health Power of Attorney

Why These Documents Matter:

Medical advance directives ensure your healthcare wishes are known and followed. They provide:

  • Clear direction for healthcare providers
  • Peace of mind for you and your family
  • Avoidance of family disagreements about care
  • Legal authority for trusted agents
