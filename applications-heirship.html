Applications for Heirship

Establishing legal heirs when a loved one dies without a will

When a loved one dies without a will, Sharon helps families get the determination of heirship needed to settle the estate.

When You Need This:

  • Deceased person left no valid will
  • Need to establish legal heirs
  • Real estate needs to be transferred
  • Bank accounts or other assets require heir verification
  • Family relationships need legal clarification

The Process:

A court proceeding where we present evidence to establish who the legal heirs are under Texas law:

  • Gather family history and documentation
  • Prepare and file the application
  • Present evidence to the court
  • Obtain the official judgment of heirship
Timeline Expectation

This process is highly variable as it mandates the involvement of additional parties who are not bound by any timeline (3 to 8 months or more)
