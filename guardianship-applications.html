Guardianship Applications

Legal assistance with guardianship applications for proper care and protection

Legal assistance with guardianship applications to ensure proper care and protection for those who need it most.

When Guardianship is Needed:

  • Adult with mental or physical incapacity
  • Minor child without suitable parents
  • Person unable to make financial decisions
  • Individual requiring medical decision support
  • Emergency protective situations

Our Support:

Guardianship proceedings can be complex and emotionally challenging. We provide:

  • Thorough evaluation of guardianship needs
  • Preparation of all required documentation
  • Court representation throughout the process
  • Ongoing compliance and reporting assistance
Timeline Expectation
1 Assessment & documentation preparation (2-4 weeks)
2 Court filing & initial hearing (3-6 weeks)
3 Final hearing & appointment (2-4 weeks)
