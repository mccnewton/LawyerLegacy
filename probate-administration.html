Probate

Guiding families through the probate process with compassion and expertise

Guiding families through the probate process with compassion, making it as simple and painless as possible.

The Process:

  • Initial consultation and case assessment
  • Filing probate application with the court
  • Inventory and appraisal of estate assets
  • Admittance of the Will to probate
  • Appointment of the executor by the court
  • Notice to beneficiaries and creditors
  • Final distribution to beneficiaries

Sharon's Approach:

Having experienced the loss of her own spouse, Sharon understands the emotional and practical challenges families face during probate. She focuses on:

  • Clear communication throughout the process
  • Handling legal paperwork so you can focus on healing
  • Minimizing court appearances when possible
  • Expediting the process while ensuring accuracy
