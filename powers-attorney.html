Powers of Attorney

Durable powers of attorney for financial matters and healthcare decisions

Durable powers of attorney for financial matters and healthcare decisions to protect you and your family.

Types Available:

  • Durable Power of Attorney for Financial Affairs
  • Medical Power of Attorney
  • Directive to Physicians (Living Will)
  • HIPAA Authorization
  • Limited or Specific Powers of Attorney

Peace of Mind:

Powers of Attorney ensure your wishes are followed if you become unable to make decisions:

  • Avoid costly guardianship proceedings
  • Ensure trusted people can act on your behalf
  • Make healthcare decisions according to your values
  • Protect your financial interests
